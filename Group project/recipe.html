Vegetables

Here are some healthy, low-carb salad recipes according to your choice of vegetables.

Antipasto-Salad
Ingredients Used:

8 cups chopped romaine lettuce, 3/4 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, halved 6 ounces provolone cheese, diced 4 ounces marinated artichokes, drained and chopped 1/2 cup olives ((about 3 ounces)).

$7.99
Creamy-Classic-Chicken-Salad
Ingredients Used:

12 oz cooked chicken breast, (shredded or diced (about 2½ cups)), 2 stalks of celery, diced ((about 1 cup)), 3 green onions, sliced ¼ cup craisins (dried cranberries), ½ cup mayonnaise.

$10.99
Green Salad
Ingredients Used:

1 recipe Homemade Italian Dressing, 1 Romaine heart (3 cups), 5 cups leafy lettuce, 1 large shallot, 1 English cucumber (2 cups chopped).

$6.99
Healthy-Spinach-Salad
Ingredients Used:

11 oz box of organic baby spinach (pre-washed), 4 hard boiled eggs (sliced), 1 cup grape tomatoes (cut in halves), 1 long English cucumber (sliced), 2 cups mushrooms (sliced).

$7.50
Taco-Salad
Ingredients Used:

1 recipe of taco meat, 6 cups romaine lettuce, chopped (1 large head), 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 3/4 cup shredded cheese and 1 avocado (sliced).

$11.75
Mediterranean-Chopped-Salad
Ingredients Used:

2 heads romaine lettuce (chopped, about 6 cups), 1 cup grape tomatoes (cut in half), 1 cup Persian cucumbers (chopped), 15 ounce can chickpeas ( drained and rinsed), ¼ cup red onion (finely chopped).

$7.25
Recipedia-Special-Salad
Ingredients Used:

½ cup peeled and diced carrot, ½ cup diced cucumber, ½ cup shredded red cabbage, ½ cup diced beetroot, ½ cup diced sweet pepper.

$8.50
Classic-Broccoli-Salad
Ingredients Used:

4 1/2 cups fresh broccoli, cut into small florets (about 2 heads of broccoli), cooked and crumbled, 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped 2/3 cup dried cranberries ((reduced sugar would work too)), 1/2 cup sliced almonds.

$7.99
